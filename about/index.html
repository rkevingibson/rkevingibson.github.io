about

I am a PhD student at the University of Toronto working in the Dynamic Graphics Project (DGP), under the supervision of Karan Singh. I graduated from Queen’s University in 2014 with a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Engineering, in the Computing and Communications option.

I’m interested in a variety of areas of computer graphics, particularly in the creation of more convincing digital humans. Outside of research I’m also interested in real time graphics and animation, as well as music.
